# Getting Started (Students)

## 1. Prerequisites
- A WireGuard profile from your instructor (or confirmed source-IP allowlist).
- A testing host with standard tooling.
- Read and accept `rules-of-engagement.md` first.

## 2. Connect
1. Import the WireGuard profile and connect (or confirm allowlist access).
2. Verify you can reach your assigned entry point and **only** your assigned scope.

## 3. Scope
- Team: `team1`
- In-scope CIDRs / entry points: provided at deploy.

## 4. Objectives & flags
- Objectives released per attack path under `docs/student/objectives/` (later).
- Dynamic flags (per-team) are submitted to the scoring system; static evidence objectives
  are graded from your report.

## 5. Methodology (NIST SP 800-115 aligned)
Recon/mapping -> hypotheses & test cases -> validation (minimal, evidence-producing) ->
lateral progression as objectives authorize -> evidence & reporting.

## 6. Report template (submit as PDF)
Executive summary; scope & authorization; methodology; findings (per the RoE finding
template); evidence appendix; remediation summary.
